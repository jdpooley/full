Typical ways to work with me:

Strategy Workshops

Today's digital landscape is full of interesting and complex challenges. Face-to-face strategy sessions allow us to examine the most pressing issues facing your business. We usually start by auditing where you are and how you operate in order to uncover any issues. I bring research to the table and challenge assumptions in order to identify new opportunities. The end result is a tangible roadmap to get your business to a desired future state.

Embedded Consultancy

Joining your team on-site for the project duration, I address specific problems in targeted sprints. This usually takes place following an initial strategy workshop in which I’ve identified issues and proposed solutions. Projects can vary in scope from full marketing strategy development including market research & positioning down to execution. Typically, I’m hired to help companies to develop new marketing capabilities in areas such as SEO and Digital PR.

Developing Digital Teams

Good marketing talent is hard to find. With extensive experience in hiring, training and leading effective teams and an in-depth knowledge of the digital landscape, I can support you in defining organisational needs and resourcing accordingly.
